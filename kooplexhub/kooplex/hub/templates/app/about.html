Kooplex is developed by the Kooplex Team

It's purpose is to give easy access to large databases, facilitate collaboration between members and groups and explore effective workflows in research

Please ask a fellow user for help or write to kooplex at complex.elte.hu
  • To reach the developers, please send a message to kooplex at complex.elte.hu.
  • To report problems or buggy features, please, file an issue at https://github.com/kooplex/kooplex-hub
  • There is manual at https://github.com/kooplex/kooplex-hub/wiki
